Asthma Information

What is Asthma?

Asthma is a chronic respiratory condition characterized by inflammation of the airways, leading to difficulty in breathing.

Symptoms

  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest tightness
  • Coughing, especially at night or early morning
  • Wheezing

Risk Factors

  • Family history of asthma or allergies
  • Exposure to tobacco smoke
  • Environmental factors (pollution, allergens)
  • Respiratory infections during childhood

What You Can Do

  • Use prescribed inhalers or medications as directed
  • Avoid triggers like smoke and allergens
  • Regular exercise to maintain lung health
  • Have a written asthma action plan
