Hepatitis Information

What is Hepatitis?

Hepatitis is an inflammation of the liver, and it can be caused by viruses, medications, toxins, or autoimmune conditions. Viral hepatitis is the most common cause.

Types of Viral Hepatitis

  • Hepatitis A
  • Hepatitis B
  • Hepatitis C
  • Hepatitis D
  • Hepatitis E

Symptoms

  • Fatigue
  • Jaundice (yellowing of the skin and eyes)
  • Abdominal pain
  • Nausea and vomiting

Risk Factors

  • Contaminated food or water (Hepatitis A and E)
  • Unprotected sex and sharing of needles (Hepatitis B and C)
  • Close contact with an infected person
  • Travel to regions with high hepatitis prevalence
  • Having multiple sexual partners
  • Receiving contaminated blood or blood products

What You Can Do

  • Vaccination (Hepatitis A and Hepatitis B)
  • Practicing safe hygiene and sanitation
  • Antiviral medications for some types
  • Lifestyle and dietary modifications
